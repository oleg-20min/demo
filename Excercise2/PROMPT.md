# Exercise 2: Document Analysis

These are eviction notices issued by the Assam state government in India,
written in Assamese script. Your task: analyze and translate them.

## Prompt

```
I have 4 eviction notice images (notice_1.jpg through notice_4.jpg) and a
consolidated PDF (nagaon_eviction_notices_consolidated.pdf), all in Assamese.

For each notice:
1. Translate the full text to English, preserving structure (header, addressee,
   body, legal references, dates, signatures)
2. Extract: who issued it, who it's addressed to, what action is required,
   by what date, and under what legal authority
3. Note anything procedurally unusual or legally significant

Then write a summary comparing all 4 notices: are they identical templates
or do they differ? What patterns do you see?
```
